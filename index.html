Your One Stop for Boba Reviews

Tempe Tea Time helps to provide an in depth review of the local Asian tea places (aka boba tea) in and around Tempe, specifically catering to Arizona State University (ASU) students.

History of Boba Tea

Boba tea originates from Taiwan in Tainan and Taichung around the 1980's. Typical boba tea contains a tea, milk or creamer, a sweetener, and toppings. Toppings can include anything from tapioca balls, coconut jelly, pudding, and many more. Boba also is known as bubble tea and pearl milk tea.

What we do?

Here at Tempe Tea Time, we aim to help direct customers to the best boba tea locations for the best experience and to support the businesses that deserve the credit. Boba is an increasingly popular phenomenon that drive many business owners to open sub-par boba places that are a disgrace to boba and everthing it represents.

Rating Criteria

We rate the boba locations based off of these factors:

  • Environment (aesthetic and design)
  • Parking
  • Seating
  • Price (depending on sizes)
  • Tea quality
  • Boba quality
  • Dairy-free option
  • Size varieties
  • Topping options
  • Food (if offered)
  • Rewards System
  • Wifi
  • Study worthy
  • Overall impression

DISCLAIMER!

The ratings for each criteria is based on opinions of several "researchers" and then averaged in an attempt to create a less biased opinion. Take these reviews with a grain of salt.
